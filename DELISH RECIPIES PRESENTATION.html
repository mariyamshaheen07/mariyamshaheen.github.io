Chicken Karahi

Main Ingredients

  • Chicken – 1 kg
  • Tomatoes – 4 (chopped)
  • Onion – 1 (optional)
  • Green chilies – 4–5
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Cooking oil – ½ cup

Spices

  • Red chili powder – 1½ tsp
  • Crushed red chilies – 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Black pepper – ½ tsp
  • Garam masala – ½ tsp
  • Salt – to taste

Garnish

  • Fresh coriander
  • Julienned ginger
  • Lemon wedges

Daal Chawal

Daal

  • Masoor / Moong daal – 1 cup
  • Water – 4 cups
  • Onion – 1 (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tsp
  • Cooking oil – 3 tbsp

Spices

  • Turmeric – ½ tsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Cumin seeds – ½ tsp
  • Salt – to taste

Rice

  • Basmati rice – 1 cup
  • Water – 2 cups
  • Salt – ½ tsp

Nihari

Main Ingredients

  • Beef or mutton – 1 kg
  • Onions – 2 (sliced)
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Oil or ghee – ½ cup
  • Water or stock – 6–7 cups

Spices

  • Nihari masala – 2–3 tbsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric – ½ tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Wheat flour (atta) – 2 tbsp

Garnish

  • Fresh ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Fresh coriander
  • Lemon wedges
